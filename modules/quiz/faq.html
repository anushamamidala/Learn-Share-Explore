FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Is there a time limit to complete the courses?
No, you can complete the courses according to your convenience.
2. Is there any certification issued upon successful completion of courses?
Yes, but for that student need to register for tests and score atleast 75 percent in both theory and practicle.
3. Do I need to pay for courses?
No, but you need to register your account in order to avail full courses.
4. Do I need to pay for certification tests?
Yes, you would have to pay for certification tests.
5. Can I get extented help on unclear topics? If yes, how?
There is a student-tutor interaction section where tutors would help students to clear all of their doubts. You would need to ask questions about a subject and your questions would be answered by respective teachers.
6. Are these courses helpful?
Yes, these courses will help you improve and stand in a more strong position in your feild.
7. What, if I don't clear my tests?
YOu can retake the courses online as these do not have physical time limits to be completed and carry on with your own convenience.
8. Can I be tutor and student at the same time?
Yes, ofcourse. But you can't be a tutor and a student for the same course at a particular time since you yourself are taking classes on the same subject.
9. Do I need to make different profiles if I am a student as well a tutor on this platform?
Yes, you would have to make two different profiles because there would be a lot of diffrent content which you don't want to get confused of.
10. Will our experience as teachers count in outer world?
If students give positive feedback and strongly refer you to other students thereby making your students list longer making you popular , you can definitely use this experience in physical world as this is a recognised platform affiliated to International Education Standard.
