FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Is there a time limit to complete the courses?

No, you can complete the courses according to your convenience.

2. Is there any certification issued upon successful completion of courses?

Yes, but for that student need to register for tests and score atleast 75 percent in both theory and practicle.

3. Do I need to pay for courses?

No, but you need to register your account in order to avail full courses.

4. Do I need to pay for certification tests?

Yes, you would have to pay for certification tests.

5. Can I get extented help on unclear topics? If yes, how?

There is a student-tutor interaction section where tutors would help students to clear all of their doubts. You would need to ask questions about a subject and your questions would be answered by respective teachers.

6. Are the classes here collaborate with the courses we have at our institutes?

Topics remain same no matter where the course is taught. You can no doubt continue classes at both ends without much confusion.

7. Where are the certifications valid?

Certificaions are valid everywhere,i.e., you can put the certifications earned here on your resume or CV.

8. How do the tutors earn since all the courses are free of cost?

Tutors here teach out of their interest, practically they don't earn money through teaching on this platform but yes, they do gain important experience which they can use for future references.

9. Can an individual be the student and tutor at the same time?

Yes, definitely. An individual can be a student of a subject but can teach the subjects they expertise in (other than the one they are currently learning).

10. Do tutors help and/or give projects for on-hand experience upon the completion of the course.

Yes, students are given projects to implement their skills. Tutors freehandedly, help students to complete their projects in such a way so that students can showcase them as a bonus point in their future.
